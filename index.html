Common Questions

Before you start

The questions I get asked most, answered straight.

How do I start a thrift business?

The truth: you just have to start. But first, go for knowledge — learn, learn, learn. Understanding sourcing and pricing before you spend a naira is what separates people who build a real business from people who lose money on their first bale.

Can I start with a full bale?

Of course — but you need to be properly trained to understand sorting and pricing of a full bale as a beginner, so you don't run into loss. This is exactly what the starter class walks you through before you commit money to a bale.

Can I start with ₦20,000?

Yes, you can. Items like bralettes, bumshorts, and singlets are advised if your capital is ₦20k — you can easily double your capital with those items.

Can I start with a "boutique standard" bale?

There's no such thing as a "boutique standard" bale — that's not a real grading term. What you actually want to ask for is a Grade AA or Grade AB bale.

How many pieces are in a bale?

A bale is weighed in kg, not counted in pieces. Depending on how heavy each item is, you can get around 100 pieces in a sweatshirt bale, 200 or more in a top bale, or about 150 in a dress bale.
